Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 22, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Open Tuner ("we", "our", or "us") handles user information.

1. Information We Collect

Open Tuner does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

The app may request access to your device's microphone solely for the purpose of detecting sound frequency to tune instruments. This audio data is processed in real-time and is not recorded, stored, or transmitted.

2. Permissions

The app may request the following permission:

  • Microphone: Used only for real-time audio processing to detect pitch.

3. Data Usage

Since no personal data is collected, no data is used, stored, or shared by the app.

4. Third-Party Services

Open Tuner does not use third-party services that collect user data.

5. Open Source

This app is open-source. You can review the code to verify how data is handled:

https://github.com/ideogram/open-tuner

6. Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect any data from children under 13 years of age.
